01

Online in minutes. Zero assembly.

Field deployment
STEP 1

Set it down. Keep it closed.

Lay the case flat anywhere with open sky — the label shows which face points up. The satellite antenna connects right through the sealed case. Rain, dust, snow: it stays shut.

STEP 2

Power on

One switch on the sealed side panel wakes the battery, the satellite terminal, and the Wi-Fi. The power display confirms everything — % and hours remaining — without opening a latch.

STEP 3

Do normal things

Join the Wi-Fi and carry on: call your family, check the fire map, send the report, take the video call — hundreds of kilometres from the nearest cell tower.

Know your runway, to the hour.

Power readout

The worst moment to guess your remaining power is the moment it matters. SatPack Go doesn't make you guess.

  • Glance at the side panel: battery % and hours remaining, live — plan the night instead of hoping
  • It counts every milliamp in and out (a true hardware fuel gauge, not a voltage guess)
  • Charging or full? The display says so — the case stays shut
  • Cools itself when it's working hard; between uses it sleeps for months and stays ready
