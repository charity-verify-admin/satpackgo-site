Step / 01

Set It Down. Keep It Closed.

Lay the case flat anywhere with open sky — the label shows which face points up. The satellite antenna connects right through the sealed case. Rain, dust, snow: it stays shut.

Step / 02

Power On

One switch on the sealed side panel wakes the battery, the satellite terminal, and the onboard Wi-Fi. The power display confirms everything — % and hours remaining — without opening a latch.

Connect

Join the SatPack Wi-Fi network from your phone or laptop. High-speed, low-latency satellite internet — hundreds of kilometres from the nearest cell tower.

Power Telemetry

Know your runway,
to the hour.

In an emergency, guessing your remaining power is not an option. SatPack Go's custom monitoring board tracks every milliamp in and out of the battery at the hardware level.

  • Hardware coulomb counting — a true fuel gauge, not a voltage guess
  • Live battery percentage and hours-remaining on the built-in OLED
  • Charge detection with charging indicator and full-charge sync
  • Automatic temperature-controlled ventilation
  • Deep-sleep standby — the monitor sips microamps when the kit is off
