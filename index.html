09

Why it exists.

From the builder

Cell towers fail exactly when you need them most. And the gear that's supposed to save you usually needs assembling, plugging in, and babying — in the rain, in the dark, in a hurry.

So I built the opposite: a case you set on the ground and switch on. The antenna works through the lid. The battery is inside. The readout is on the outside. It just works.
